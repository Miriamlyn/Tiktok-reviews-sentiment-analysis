# TIKTOK REVIEWS SENTIMENT ANALYSIS
## by Chimamaka Chilaka


## Dataset

> The dataset was gotten from Kaggle.

## Installation
The libraries/packages used for this project includes;

Pandas: For Assessing and manipulating libraries
Matplotlib: for Visualization
Seaborn: for Visualization
Wordcloud: for WordCloud representation
Textblob: For sentiment analysis

## About the project
The project analyses the reviews of the Tiktok app on Google playstore and the sentiments associated with the review.

## Summary of Findings
In conclusion, Tiktok may be a thriving app, but this doe not mean that all reviews are positive. The above shows that there are people that do not like the app even though the people that love the app are more than those that dislike the app.
